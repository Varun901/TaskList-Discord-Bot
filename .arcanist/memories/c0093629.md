The `daily_digest` background task in `bot.py` originally used an exact-minute check (`now.hour == DAILY_POST_HOUR and now.minute == DAILY_POST_MINUTE`), creating a 1-minute window that could be silently missed on bot restarts, reconnects, or asyncio scheduling jitter. The fix introduces a module-level `_last_digest_date: Optional[date]` variable. Each loop tick now checks whether the post time has been reached AND the digest hasn't been sent today, meaning a late bot start or skipped tick will still fire the digest on the next available tick rather than silently dropping it for the entire day.
